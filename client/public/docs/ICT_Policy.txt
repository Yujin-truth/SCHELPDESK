ICT Policy

1. Acceptable Use
   - University IT resources are for academic purposes.
   - Personal use is limited.

2. Security
   - Use strong passwords.
   - Report suspicious activity immediately.

3. Data Protection
   - Do not share personal data.
   - Comply with GDPR and local laws.

4. Internet Usage
   - No illegal downloads.
   - Respect bandwidth limits.

For technical support, use the help desk system.
