Student Handbook

Welcome to the Smart Campus Help Desk System.

This handbook outlines the policies and procedures for students at our university.

1. Academic Integrity
   - Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
   - All assignments must be original work.

2. Code of Conduct
   - Respect fellow students and staff.
   - Maintain a positive learning environment.

3. IT Policies
   - Use university computers responsibly.
   - Do not share passwords.

For more information, contact the help desk.
