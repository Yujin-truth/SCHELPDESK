Examination Regulations

1. Exam Scheduling
   - Exams are scheduled at the beginning of each semester.
   - Check the student portal for dates.

2. Conduct During Exams
   - No cheating or unauthorized materials.
   - Phones must be turned off.

3. Appeals
   - Appeals must be submitted within 7 days.
   - Contact academic affairs for procedures.

Violations may result in disciplinary action.
